What is WebApp

TCDSE is a web application designed to conduct computational tasks to generate information needed for decision mechanisms in designing future cities. The web application, which will be referred as TCDSE for short, contains a computational engine capable of executing several hazard scenarios on different exposure datasets and infrastructures. This platform is developed within the scope of the Tomorrow's Cities project. For project details, please click here.

Who is WebApp for

TCDSE is intended for urban planners, municipal and regional decision-makers, researchers, and infrastructure practitioners who need to explore how multiple hazards may affect assets and people under future scenarios. It supports evidence-based planning by enabling users to compare scenarios, test assumptions, and communicate results to stakeholders in a consistent, transparent way.
